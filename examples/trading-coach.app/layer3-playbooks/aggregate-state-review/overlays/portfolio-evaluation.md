# Overlay — portfolio-evaluation

Layer 2 **evaluation** overlay.

Runs when `evaluation: true` on aggregate-state-review playbook.

**Uses:** `evaluation-entry-interview`, `exit-interview` (see `.asp.yaml`).

When `thematic: true`: score thesis consistency, catalyst alignment, horizon discipline, rotation quality, liquidity policy.

When `thematic: false`: assess construction quality, diversification, category drift, liquidity policy.

Includes entry interview (distinct from map confirmation), scorecard, verdict, exit interview.
