# Overlay — rebalancing-review

Layer 2 **enrichment** overlay. Active when `rebalancingReview: true`.

## Quantification additions

Add to Report.md:

- Portfolio Rebalancing Scoreboard
- Sources Of Capital / Uses Of Capital
- Additions And Reductions
- Concentration Review
- Liquidity And Quality Review
- Rebalancing Versus Churn

## Evaluation additions (when `evaluation: true`)

Entry interview themes:

- intended portfolio objective for the period
- target allocation or target risk posture if known
- positions to reduce, increase, or eliminate
- whether rebalancing was planned or opportunistic
- whether proceeds funded better ideas, cash, income, hedges
- whether concentration and liquidity improved or worsened
- whether any position remained because of inertia rather than thesis
- portfolio rule for next period

Assessment: concentration, quality, liquidity, diversification, cash optionality, stale holdings, position sizing, alignment with market environment.

Scorecard facets: construction quality, concentration control, liquidity, capital redeployment quality, role clarity, risk alignment, next-period readiness.
