# Overlay — risk-review

Layer 2 **enrichment** overlay. Active when `riskReview: true`.

## Quantification additions

Add to Report.md:

- Risk Rotation Scoreboard
- Market Risk Regime
- Buy/Sell Flow Review
- Realized Gain Harvesting Versus True De-Risking
- Stale-Risk Review
- Concentration And Exposure Review

## Evaluation additions (when `evaluation: true`)

Entry interview themes:

- intended risk posture at start and end of period
- target cash level, equity exposure, or gross exposure
- whether raising cash, lowering beta, reducing concentration, upgrading quality
- which sells were risk reduction versus profit harvesting
- whether proceeds retained as cash or redeployed
- max tolerable single-name exposure and stale-loser rules
- retirement positioning, liquidity needs, capital preservation drivers
- risk rule for next period

Assessment: whether period improved risk posture; measurable risk plan presence; alignment with environment.

Scorecard facets: risk direction, risk-plan clarity, exposure reduction quality, concentration control, stale-risk cleanup, capital redeployment discipline, next-period governance.
